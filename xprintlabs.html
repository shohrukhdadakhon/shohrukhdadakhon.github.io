What Is X Print Labs?

At X Print Labs, we're making 3D printing easy and accessible for all students. Also this project is our Bachelor’s Thesis.

Students can print their 3D parts from anywhere using our simple web application. They can customize their print settings easily to meet their specific needs.

Students can also watch their projects being printed in real-time, no matter where they are. Our platform ensures that students can stay connected to their work from start to finish.
